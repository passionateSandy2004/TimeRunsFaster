— TWO COMPETING EFFECTS —

Einstein discovered that two things change how fast time flows:


⚡ Special Relativity Moving clocks tick slower. The satellite moves much faster than you, so its clock loses time.


🌍 General Relativity Clocks in weaker gravity tick faster. The satellite is far from Earth where gravity is weaker, so its clock gains time.


The gravity effect wins — satellites end up running faster than your clock. Without correcting for this, your phone's maps would be wrong by kilometres every single day.
